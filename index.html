About Me

Nicky Hervyan is a Videographer and Photographer from Indonesia. He discovered his passion for videography in his teenage years, and his interest in photography developed shortly after. He honed his skills in photography and videography by taking random photographs and videos as practice. After graduating school, he worked on small projects with a team of seven people, and eventually landed larger corporate, restaurant, and wedding projects. Over the years, Nicky has built a successful career in videography and photography, and continues to create stunning visuals for his clients.
